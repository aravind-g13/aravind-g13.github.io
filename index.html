General Rules

  • Kindly adhere to deadlines in registration and submissions. Late entries will not be considered.
  • Please present yourselves in formal attire on the day of the event.
  • Kindly ensure that you have a stable internet connection and a fully functioning camera and microphone on your device. Please try to resolve any technical issues that could hamper with your participation during the events.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Whom can I contact in case of queries regarding my event?

Kindly contact the event co-ordinators / chairperson

2. What can I do if there is a clash in event timings?

Kindly communicate these details to the organisers a day in advance positively. Participants who fail to do so cannot be helped on the day of the event.

3. Can I make changes to my details after submitting the form?

Any changes/corrections should be informed on the day of the event.

4. Can I change my team after registration?

Change in teams and members will not be entertained. Please choose your teams carefully

5. How will I know if my registration is successful?

Successfully registered participants will receive a verification email from the organisers. If not check the mail has reached the spam folder. IElse, you would have entered a wrong email ID. Kindly contact the chairpersons
